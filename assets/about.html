ABOUT US

Welcome to Divide By Zero™! Originally born as a humble Clash of Clans alliance, we have since formed a separate thriving Discord gaming server that embraces a wide range of gaming interests. Our journey began with a shared passion for conquering virtual realms and building a strong alliance. As the community grew, so did our horizons, and now, we stand as a united force, breaking barriers and defying limits in countless gaming universes. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a fresh-faced newcomer, Divide by Zero is the perfect haven to connect with like-minded gamers, forge new friendships, and embark on unforgettable adventures together. We have 7000+ members in our CoC alliance server and 450+ members in the Gaming server, growing continuously. Join us as we continue to celebrate the spirit of camaraderie and push the boundaries of gaming excellence!
